This is a game that people play in stalls. You and the computer have been given $10,000 by default.
Next you have to enter amount of money you are going to risk. Your amount should be lesser than or 
equal to the money and that is left with you. Then you role the dice, a random number is going to
appear. If it is equal to or less than 5 then the computer will get the money you put on bet,if
the value is greater than or equal to 7 then the computer will give you the double of what you
put on bet and if the value is 6 then the computer will give you the triple of what you put on bet.
